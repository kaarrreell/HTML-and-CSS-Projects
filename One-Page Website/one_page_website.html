The Zodiac:

Zodiac signs are associated with the position of the sun at the moment of your birth.
There are 12 different signs, each with its own unique traits, strengths, weaknesses, and perspectives on life.
These signs are ruled by elements (fire, earth, air, or water) and influenced by other constellations and planets.
